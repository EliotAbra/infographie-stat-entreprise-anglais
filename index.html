Thanks to this graph, we can better understand the number of companies that have taken on interns, as well as the economic potential of the region of Normandie.

Company Status

This graph represents the proportion of different statuses of companies that hired interns in 2023.

The objective is to show that, although some are more numerous, all companies hire interns.

Business Sectors

More and more companies are seeking to digitize, sometimes in fields unrelated to the digital domain.

The skills of an IT intern can quickly prove to be very useful and indispensable.

Company Headquarter

Most companies have a headquarters, which increases credibility and can also provide strategic location to attract more clients.

However, there are companies that do not have a headquarters, which can lead to problems such as communication difficulties or even legal and tax problems.

Location of Companies

Based on the data presented above, the number of interns in Rouen is much higher than in other cities since it's where companies are more numerous.

However, there are always exceptions.
